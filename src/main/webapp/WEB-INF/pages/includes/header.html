The Travel Awards 2013 - Readers' Awards survey

For the first time ever, The Times, The Sunday Times and The Sunday Times Travel Magazine are coming together to bring you the definitive travel awards as voted for by you.

Make sure you have your say and let us know the hotels, airlines, tour operators and destinations that have made your trips magical. We need to know who you think helps to make your all-important holiday time special! By completing this survey you will automatically be placed into our prize draw for a chance of winning one of 14 dream holidays, including a once-in-a-lifetime trip for two to Australia, worth £13,000, courtesy of Scenic Tours.

To vote, simply pick your favourite from the lists below (compiled from reader feedback over the past year) or nominate your own winner. You have just one precious vote per category, so make sure you use it well. Partner Destination 2013 You may not vote for your own company or one you work for.

The awards ceremony will take place in Malta in October 2013.

We look forward to reading through your votes and good luck!

Jane Knight, Travel Editor The Times
