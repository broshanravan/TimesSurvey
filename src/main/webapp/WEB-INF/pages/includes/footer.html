• Trusted

    Feefo's feedback engine is founded on consumer loyalty and trust.


    We have defined a process to ensure that the ratings and reviews generated by Feefo can be trusted by consumers and merchants, empowering them with genuine feedback from only genuine consumers.

     

  • Independent

    Feefo works independently of merchants, on behalf of consumers.


    This provides a unique opportunity to collate and publish genuine feedback that is not moderated, or manipulated but reflects the reality of consumers' perceptions.

     

  • Definitive

    Feefo is working towards being the most trusted ratings and reviews engine in the world.


    Because of our platform and processes, we can assure consumers that the results produced are definitive. We publish reviews that are both positive and negative and then collate the ratings in to a simple score that clearly identifies consumer opinion.
